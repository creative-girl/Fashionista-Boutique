The Company

Coming from a strong experience of manufacturing for global brands, the company has its own manufacturing units with a capacity of producing over 100,000 units per month. The company excels in end-to-end supply chain solutions, from product design and development, raw material sourcing, outsourcing vendors, production management and quality control to in-country logistics, global freight management and e-logistics. The state of the art infrastructure also includes digital printing and computer embroidery machines to offer the quality of global standards. The company has its in-house design team and works with multiple vendors locally and internationally. With clear expansion plans, both locally and globally, the company is dedicated to investing in the future with the aspiration to find a place in every urban woman's wardrobe.
